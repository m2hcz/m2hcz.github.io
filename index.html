About me

I'm a senior software engineer and offensive security researcher with over a decade of hands-on experience building — and breaking — large-scale systems. My work sits at the intersection of secure software engineering and adversarial research: designing resilient backend architecture on one side, and dismantling it through vulnerability research, reverse engineering, and exploit development on the other.

I bring an investigative, results-oriented mindset to every engagement — applying strong critical thinking and an offensive security mindset to map attack surfaces, validate impact with working proofs of concept, and translate complex technical risk into clear narratives for engineering teams, business teams, and executive stakeholders.

I operate effectively in dynamic, high-pressure environments, both independently and embedded in a team, and have worked within financial / fintech-grade contexts where correctness, confidentiality, and uptime are non-negotiable. With knowledge of reverse engineering and advanced exploitation and a solid software engineering background, my focus stays the same: practical security impact backed by rigorous engineering.
